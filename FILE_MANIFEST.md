## Pages Summary

### index.html
- Fixed nav with logo, links, Donate CTA
- Split hero (text left / photo right) with animated entrance
- Red ticker band with campaign issue keywords
- About strip with 4 stat cards
- 6-card priorities grid (Fiscal, Safety, Flood, Agriculture, Housing, Veterans)
- Red pull quote band
- Donate CTA section
- Contact strip (phone, email, Facebook)
- Footer with disclaimer

### about.html
- Same nav
- Page hero banner with background watermark text
- Story section with text + photo frame (placeholder)
- Values band (Honesty, Integrity, Transparency)
- Numbered issues list (5 platform issues)
- Leadership record grid (8 roles/accomplishments)
- Red CTA section (Donate / Volunteer)
- Contact strip + footer

### endorsements.html
- Same nav
- Page hero banner
- Featured endorsements grid (3 cards: Snaza, Abbarno, Riley)
- WA Farm Bureau organizational endorsement
- Community voices grid (3 endorsers + "Add Your Voice" card)
- Support/endorse form
- Contact strip + footer

### contact.html
- Same nav
- Page hero banner
- Quick contact cards row (Phone, Email, Facebook)
- Side-by-side Contact form + Volunteer form
- Donate section with amount buttons + contribution form
- Yard sign request CTA band
- Contact strip + footer
